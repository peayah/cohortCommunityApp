About

Free office hours for programming help - any programming language, all skill levels. The format is super simple: you come with a laptop, sit, hang out, and code, and flag down one of the leaders if you have a question. That's it! If we can't answer your question, we'll find you someone who can, or at least point you in the right direction.

More experienced folks are also welcome as mentors.

By becoming a member, you agree to abide by and uphold our policies.
